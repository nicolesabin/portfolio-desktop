Musician Documentary

Videography

Skills Used:

  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Rule of Thirds
  • L-Cuts & J-Cuts
  • Audio Mixing

Project Overview

This multimedia project showcases my video production and editing skills using Adobe Premiere Pro. The documentary captures the story of a local music artist from Logan, Utah, demonstrating my ability to tell compelling narratives through video.

Project Details

This project involved the complete video production process from filming to final edit:

  • Video capture and cinematography using the Rule of Thirds composition technique
  • Professional editing in Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Advanced editing techniques including L-Cuts and J-Cuts for smooth transitions
  • Audio mixing and sound design
  • Storytelling through visual narrative

Key Achievements

This documentary project demonstrates proficiency in both the technical and creative aspects of video production. The use of professional editing techniques like L-Cuts and J-Cuts, combined with proper composition and audio mixing, results in a polished, engaging documentary that effectively tells the musician's story.
